Daisies

Time is an enemy,
Longing for her everyday is torture.


Her smile is as cozy as the morning sunlight damping on my skin.
Pretty as daisies, that gives me joy beyond this world.


Her touch gentle as cotton
And a laugh that even the universe will be glad to hear.


I don't want to meet your gaze
Captured, Trapped in your eyes, Im a prisoner.


I am nothing but a fool, but for you I will be better.
So come with me, and let's conquer the world together.
